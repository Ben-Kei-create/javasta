Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Javasta ("the App") is a Java SE certification exam preparation app developed by Fumiaki Motegi ("the Developer"). This policy explains how the App handles information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information.
Data required for the App's operation (study progress, answer history, settings, etc.) is stored only on your device or in iCloud (Key-Value Storage). This data is never transmitted to the Developer's servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • Displaying study progress, accuracy rates, and streak days
  • Managing the review queue and bookmarks
  • Syncing data across devices via iCloud
  • Showing data in Home Screen widgets

3. Information Sharing

The Developer does not sell, share, or disclose your data to third parties.

4. Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use advertising networks or third-party analytics SDKs. It is distributed exclusively through the App Store and relies only on Apple's standard purchase and crash-reporting mechanisms.

5. In-App Purchases

Premium plan purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Payment information is managed by Apple; the Developer has no access to card details or payment data. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from such users.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will post the updated version on this page. Significant changes will also be communicated through app updates.
